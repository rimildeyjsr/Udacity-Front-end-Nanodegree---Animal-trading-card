Lion

Even though the lion is called the 'king of the jungle', it mostly inhabits grasslands and plains.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 1.7-2.5 metres (male) 1.4-1.8 metres(female)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Habitat: grasslands and plains

Renowned for its majesty and nicknamed "the king of the jungle", the lion possesses both beauty and strength. Mature male lions are unique among big cats due the thick brown or black manes that encircle their necks and protect them while fighting.
